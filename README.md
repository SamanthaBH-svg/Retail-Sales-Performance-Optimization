# Retail-Sales-Performance-Optimization
Business analysis project focused on identifying sales inconsistencies and improving performance through data-driven insights and dashboard design
Retail Sales Performance Optimization

Project Overview
This project focuses on identifying and addressing sales inconsistencies across retail stores using a data-driven approach. A centralized dashboard was developed to provide visibility into performance across regions, stores, and products, enabling more informed and proactive decision-making.

Business Problem
The business experienced inconsistent sales performance with limited visibility into the underlying drivers. This resulted in reactive decision-making and lack of alignment across Sales, Marketing, and Finance teams.

Approach
Conducted analysis of current (AS-IS) business processes
Designed an improved (TO-BE) process to enhance visibility and alignment
Defined business and functional requirements through a structured BRD
Built a Power BI dashboard to centralize performance tracking

Solution
The final solution is an interactive Power BI dashboard that provides:
KPI tracking (Revenue, COGS, Units Sold)
Revenue trend analysis over time
Regional and store-level performance insights
Product performance breakdown
Identification of top and bottom performing stores and products
