#  Retail Sales Performance Optimization

---

###  Business Analysis Project  
Focused on identifying sales inconsistencies and improving performance through data-driven insights and dashboard design.

---

##  Project Overview
This project focuses on identifying and addressing sales inconsistencies across retail stores using a data-driven approach.

A centralized dashboard was developed to provide visibility into performance across:
- Regions  
- Stores  
- Products  

Enabling more informed and proactive decision-making.

---

##  Business Problem
The business experienced inconsistent sales performance with limited visibility into underlying drivers.

This resulted in:
- Reactive decision-making  
- Lack of alignment across Sales, Marketing, and Finance teams  

---

##  Approach
- Conducted analysis of current (AS-IS) business processes  
- Designed improved (TO-BE) processes  
- Defined business and functional requirements (BRD)  
- Built a Power BI dashboard to centralize performance tracking  

---

##  Solution
An interactive Power BI dashboard providing:

- KPI tracking (Revenue, COGS, Units Sold)  
- Revenue trend analysis  
- Regional and store-level performance insights  
- Product performance breakdown  
- Identification of top and bottom performing stores and products
